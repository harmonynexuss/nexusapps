Privacy Policy for Tile Rush

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

Company: Harmony Nexus

App Name: Tile Rush

App ID: com.nexus.tile_rush

Introduction

Harmony Nexus ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Tile Rush mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use Tile Rush, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game statistics, high scores, level progress, and gameplay patterns
  • Performance Data: App crashes, technical diagnostics, and performance metrics

Information You Provide

  • Game Progress: Your high scores and game statistics are stored locally on your device
  • Settings Preferences: Sound and vibration settings are saved locally
  • Customer Support: Information you provide when contacting our support team

Information from Third Parties

We do not currently collect information from third-party services. However, if you choose to interact with third-party services through our app (such as app stores for ratings), those interactions are governed by the third party's privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App functionality
  • Save your game progress and high scores locally on your device
  • Improve user experience and game performance
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or bugs
  • Analyze app usage to enhance features

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Game data (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not currently upload your game data to cloud servers
  • Data Persistence: Your data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data

Third-Party Services

Tile Rush may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by Harmony Nexus. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Currently, the app may link to:

  • Google Play Store (for more apps and ratings)
  • Our developer page
  • Customer support resources

Children's Privacy

Tile Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the data we store
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (uninstall the app to remove all local data)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-Out: Disable certain features through the app's settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Harmony Nexus

Email: support@harmonynexus.com

Website: https://harmonynexuss.github.io/nexusapps/

Data Retention

  • Game Data: Retained locally on your device until app uninstallation
  • Support Inquiries: Retained for as long as necessary to provide support and resolve issues

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California users
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Permissions

Tile Rush may request the following permissions:

  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback during gameplay (can be disabled in settings)
  • Storage: To save game progress and settings locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and can be managed through your device settings.
