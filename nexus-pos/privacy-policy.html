Privacy Policy for NexusPOS

Effective Date: October 13, 2025

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NexusPOS ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Harmony Nexus collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our NexusPOS mobile application (App ID: com.zerobyte.pos).

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using NexusPOS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for and use NexusPOS, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Business Information: Business name, business address
  • Authentication Data: Password (encrypted), Firebase authentication tokens

2.2 Business Data

  • Inventory Data: Product names, categories, prices, stock quantities, images
  • Sales Transactions: Transaction details, customer information, payment methods
  • Customer Information: Customer names, phone numbers, addresses, credit history
  • Financial Records: Payment records, accounting data, receipts

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, timestamps, error logs
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device using SQLite database

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Application Functions

  • Providing point-of-sale functionality
  • Managing inventory and stock control
  • Processing and recording sales transactions
  • Managing customer relationships and credit accounts
  • Generating receipts and reports
  • Accounting and financial tracking

3.2 Account Management

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Authenticating users via Firebase Authentication
  • Syncing user profiles across devices
  • Managing account settings and preferences

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing app usage to improve features
  • Debugging and fixing technical issues
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Developing new features

3.4 Communication

  • Sending transactional notifications
  • Providing customer support
  • Sharing important updates about the app

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Most data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Local data is accessible only to you through the app
  • You can create backups of your local data

4.2 Cloud Storage

  • User account information is stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Authentication is managed through Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS

4.3 Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using cryptographic algorithms
  • Firebase implements security rules to protect user data
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Session management with automatic timeout

4.4 Data Backup

  • Users can create local backups of their database
  • Backups are stored in the app's document directory
  • Users have full control over backup creation, restoration, and deletion
  • Backup files are user-specific and isolated

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Harmony Nexus does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and cloud database
    Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Fonts: Typography
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 Data Sharing Circumstances

We may disclose your information only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • In case of business transfer or merger (with notification)

6. User Rights and Controls

6.1 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your personal data stored in the app
  • Modify: Update your profile and business information
  • Export: Create backups of your data
  • Delete: Request account deactivation

6.2 Account Deactivation

  • You can request account deactivation from the Profile screen
  • Deactivation sets your account status to inactive
  • You will be logged out and prevented from logging in
  • Your data is preserved for potential reactivation
  • Contact support to reactivate or permanently delete your account

6.3 Data Portability

  • Export your entire database as a .db file
  • Share your data backups as needed
  • Import backups to restore your data

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Deactivated accounts and data are retained for 90 days
  • Backup Files: User-created backups are retained until manually deleted
  • Transaction Records: Retained for compliance and accounting purposes

8. Children's Privacy

NexusPOS is intended for business use and is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. By using NexusPOS, you consent to the transfer of information to countries that may have different data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of NexusPOS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Cookie Policy

NexusPOS uses local storage mechanisms (SQLite, SharedPreferences) to:

  • Maintain user sessions
  • Store app settings and preferences
  • Cache data for offline functionality

No tracking cookies or analytics cookies are used.

15. Additional Information

15.1 Firebase Services

We use Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: For user profile and settings synchronization

15.2 Offline Functionality

  • Your business data remains on your device

  • The app records payment method types (cash, card, etc.)
  • No sensitive payment credentials are stored
